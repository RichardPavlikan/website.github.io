Signing in

User can sign into the app using his Google account.


What data are collected?

When signing up, user agress to us saving some of this data. Data we collect from the users Google account: name, profile image and e-mail adress.
Why do we need to collect users name from his Google account? Users name is used to create his account. Other users can use users name to search for him in the app. Users name is also used when crediting him for the workout he shared with other users.
Why do we need to collect users image from his Google account? Users image is used as a first profile image in the app. User can later change his profile picture with any photo. After changing users photo, users Google photo reference is deleted from the database.
Why do we need to collect users e-mail from his Google account? Users e-mail is used to verify users indentity, and as a contact for the app developers in case of a feedback from the user. OTHER USERS DO NOT HAVE ACCESS TO SEE USERS E-MAIL ADRESS.

What data is shared with other users?

Other users can only see users name and profile image, which can be later changed.


Who has access to the collected data?

We DO NOT SHARE data with anyone. Only developers of the app have access to the collected data. NO 3rd party has access to the data.
