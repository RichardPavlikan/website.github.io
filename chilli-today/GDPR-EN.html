Google uses cookies for advertising, including the display and rendering of ads, their customization according to the settings of the ad, limiting the number of impressions of a particular ad, ignoring the ads and measuring the effectiveness of the ads. If you've enabled ad personalization, this setting applies to the European Economic Area, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom for 13 months and 24 months elsewhere. Some cookies measure ad performance, campaign, ad conversion rates, or auto-search and search completion. Such cookies expire 6 months after their last use.

For security purposes, cookies authenticate users, thus preventing fraud and protecting users of the website. User authentication ensures secure access to the account of the owner who created it. If necessary, the system can block the account if it evaluates a risk.
